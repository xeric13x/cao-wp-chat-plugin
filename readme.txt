== Description ==

Active Contact At Once! subscribers can easily add their chat and text options to WP sites with this simple plugin. Simply add your Merchant ID, Provider ID, whether or not you want your chat icons to display on your site.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'cao-wp-chat-plugin' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add your Merchant ID, Provider ID, and Placement IDs provided by Contact At Once! to make the chat icons dispaly on your site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

Q. Will the chat icons display without a valid ids provided by Contact At Once!?
A. No.
